About us

  • Who we are?

    Our business idea provides people a solution with high level of flexibility in which learners and teachers will use a sharing economy platform to learn and teach languages. By using Natively, people who want to improve their linguistic skills but don’t have enough time to follow fixed courses can learn easily and faster. And, people who want to use their free time to teach are allowed to do it even if they do not have a diploma.
